Welcome to Cookbook

Welcome to a very early preview version of Cookbook, the premier social recipe website that looks nothing like any other social networks! This is my project for the Code Institute Full Stack Web Development course I am doing and if you are seeing this it means you are one of the lucky few I have asked to preview the site before it is finished.
I need your help, for this project to be successful I need as many users as possible to add their own recipes to the site as well as rate and comment on others recipes. I will make a list of what I need people to test below, as sections of the site are finished more requests will be added. If you come across any issues I would really appreciate if you could let me know about the problem in as much detail as possible. I will have a formal bug reporting system put in place in the future but in the mean time you can just text me.

Thank you for your help testing out Cookbook!
